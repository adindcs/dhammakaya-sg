Activities at Dhammakaya 法身寺活动

From weekly meditation sessions to family-friendly festivals and cultural observances, our temple is a vibrant hub of spiritual and community life.

Guided Meditation Sessions

Join peaceful, instructor-led sessions that help you cultivate mindfulness, clarity, and emotional balance. Suitable for beginners and experienced practitioners alike.

Family & Youth Programs

Interactive and meaningful programs for children, teens, and families that help nurture compassion, respect, and mindful living from an early age.

Dhamma Talks & Teachings

Explore timeless Buddhist wisdom through weekly talks and reflections that make ancient teachings accessible and practical for modern life.

Community Outreach

Engage in acts of service through charity events, temple care, and mindful volunteer work. Contribute your time and energy to something meaningful.

Begin Your Meditation Journey

Experience the transformative power of meditation with our free guided sessions. No prior experience needed — just an open mind and a willingness to explore inner peace.
