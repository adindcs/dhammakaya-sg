"World Peace Comes from Inner Peace"

In Singapore, a land filled with vigour and vitality, there is a group of people carrying out the quintessential pursuits of life. Through meditation, they have found inner peace and true happiness for themselves, their families, and the society in which they reside. When they close their eyes gently, they generate a course of pure spiritual energy that subtly evolves into a great force that makes the world a better and more peaceful place.

Our Story 我们的故事

For over 20 years, Dhammakaya Centre (Singapore) has been actively sharing the practice of meditation suitable for everyday life — welcoming everyone irrespective of age, race, or religion.

Over the years, our centre has experienced remarkable growth in participants and activities. Due to space constraints at our previous rented premises, establishing a permanent home became our top priority.

In 2013, the land at 6 Sumang Walk, Punggol was awarded to DCS. After years of planning and construction, our purpose-built 5-storey centre was completed in 2017 — designed with a facade that resembles two palms placed together, a Buddhist gesture of respect, welcoming kind souls from all walks of life.

Our Journey 建筑过程简要

2013
Land tender submitted and awarded to DCS at 6 Sumang Walk, Punggol (828675).
投标榜鹅区苏芒径的土地，新加坡法身寺正式标得土地。
2014
Appointment of Architect & Engineer. Hand over of land by HDB. Purification of Land Ceremony presided by the Most Venerable Phraphromdilok.
正式委任建筑师。从建屋发展局承接土地。净化土地祈福法会。
2015
Driving of the 1st Symbolic Foundation Pile ceremony. Main tender call opened.
第一根地基柱奠基仪式。开放招标。
2016
Builder appointed and permit to carry out work granted. Construction begins.
正式委任建筑发展商。获批建筑工作准证。
2017
Building completed. Temporary Occupation Permit (TOP) received. DCS moves into its permanent home.
建筑竣工。领取临时入伙证。法身寺正式迁入永久道场。
