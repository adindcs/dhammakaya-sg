Basic Meditation Course 初级静坐班

A free 12-session guided meditation course. Open to all — no prior experience needed.

Register Online

About This Course 关于课程

Are you new to meditation and wish to learn how to meditate? If your answer is "yes", this course is for you!

Since the year 2000, Dhammakaya Centre Singapore has been providing meditation classes for anyone who wishes to learn. Conducted in Chinese, our course is taught by an experienced practitioner with more than 20 years of meditation instruction experience.

This course is designed with beginners in mind — easy and enjoyable with relaxation at the heart of the practice. Thousands of students have attested to the benefits of meditation after completing the course.

This course is provided free-of-charge with the kind support of our sponsors.

Course Details 课程详情

12 Lessons

Structured weekly lessons covering body stretching, meditation instructions, and guided practice

2 Hours Each

Each session lasts 2 hours with a comfortable mix of learning and practice

In Chinese

Conducted in Chinese (华语) by an instructor with 20+ years of experience

100% Free

No fees charged — supported entirely by generous sponsors and community donations

Benefits of Meditation 静坐的好处

Incorporating meditation into your daily routine can transform your life in many ways.

🧘

Inner Peace · 内在祥和

Find stillness and calm within, reducing anxiety and stress in your daily life

💪

Physical Health · 身体健康

Meditation relaxes the mind and body, improving overall physical well-being

😊

Happiness · 生活幸福

Discover true happiness that begins from within and radiates to your family and community

🧠

Mental Clarity · 心智清明

Reduce stress, sharpen focus and increase cognitive abilities and work productivity

❤️

Better Relationships · 人际关系

Positive change at the emotional level has a profound impact on your family and community

🌏

World Peace · 世界和平

Pure spiritual energy from meditation subtly evolves into a great force for global harmony

They firmly believe that — World Peace Comes from Inner Peace."
