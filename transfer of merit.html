About This Ceremony 关于此法会

Human life is precious as we can perform good deeds such as giving, observing precepts, and meditation in accumulation of merits. These merits will determine our afterlife. The other wondrous thing that we can do as humans is the ability to transfer our accumulated merits to departed ancestors and loved ones.

"When we dedicate merits to the deceased, those who are suffering will suffer less, those who are suffering a little will no longer suffer, those who have a little joy will receive more joy, those with lots of joy will get even more joyous. Our departed loved ones will rejoice in our merits with gratitude."

DCS holds a Transference of Merit ceremony on every 4th Sunday of the month. In this monthly ceremony, the Sangha will be chanting the Abhidhamma Scripture — the scripture the Lord Buddha delivered to His deceased mother and other heavenly beings in the second level of heaven, Tavatimsa.

During the Sangha's chanting, we calm our minds, recollect our merits and transfer them to our ancestors and departed loved ones. We sincerely welcome you to join us for this meaningful ceremony.

What to Expect 活动内容

This is a half-day program that combines meditation, offering, and the powerful Abhidhamma chanting ceremony. Whether you're coming to honor a loved one or simply to accumulate merits, you'll experience a profound sense of peace and connection. All are welcome — no prior experience or registration required. Simply arrive with an open heart.
