ABOUT THIS MERIT · 关于此功德

In 2014, Dhammakaya Centre Singapore purchased the land on which the DCS building now stands. The lease is valid for 30 years, with renewal due in 2044. With these facilities, DCS offers meditation, Dhamma talks, and programs for children, youth, and adults from all walks of life.

"Your contributions are integral to our mission and will enable DCS to renew the lease for the next thirty years. Your support will bring hope and prosperity to many."
  • Donors will be blessed with good health, wealth and prosperity in every lifetime until the Ultimate Nibbana
  • Your generosity sustains free teachings, meditation programs, and community events for decades to come
