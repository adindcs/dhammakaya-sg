ABOUT THIS MERIT · 关于此功德

Your donation goes towards light offering paying homage to the temple's Main Buddha Statue and Buddha Relics. The offered lamps are lit daily for a month.

Offering of Lights to the Lord Buddha is a sublime act. Light symbolises wisdom, illuminating our inner self and the elimination of delusion.
